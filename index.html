Salaar 🥰🥰🥰🥰🥰

Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire is a blockbuster         Indian Telugu-language epic action thriller movie released on 22 December 2023. Directed by Prashanth Neel and produced by Hombale Films, the film stars Prabhas in the titular role alongside Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan, and Jagapathi Babu. Set in the lawless, dystopian city-state of Khansaar, the story explores the deep bond and ultimate fallout between two childhood friends amid a violent battle for the throne.


Geetha Govindam 🥰🥰🥰🥰🥰

Geetha Govindam is a blockbuster 2018 Indian Telugu-language romantic comedy film written and directed by Parasuram.The film features Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna in the leading roles, turning them into one of Tollywood's most celebrated on-screen pairs. Produced by GA2 Pictures on a modest budget of ₹5 crore the movie went on to become a massive commercial success, grossing an estimated ₹132 crore worldwide.


Seethamma Vakitlo Sirimalle Chettu 🥰🥰🥰🥰🥰

Seethamma Vakitlo Sirimalle Chettu is a 2013 Indian Telugu-language family drama film directed by Srikanth Addala and produced by Dil Raju under the banner of Sri Venkateswara Creations . The film features an ensemble cast including Venkatesh, Mahesh Babu, Anjali, and Samantha Ruth Prabhu in the lead roles. Set against the backdrop of a traditional Telugu family , the story explores themes of love, relationships, and familial bonds. The film was a commercial success and received critical acclaim for its performances and storytelling.
